About Us

Keli Cristina Cleaning Services was founded in 2010 by Keli Cristina, a passionate entrepreneur with a vision to provide exceptional cleaning services that go beyond expectations. Keli's journey into the world of cleaning services was sparked by her own experiences as a young homeowner. Balancing work, family, and household chores, she understood the value of a well-kept and organized living space. Starting with a handful of clients, Keli's unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction drove the company's early growth. Word-of-mouth spread, and soon, Keli Cristina Cleaning Services became known for its quality work and trustworthiness. Upholding excellence, Keli invested in comprehensive training programs for her team members, setting high standards for professional cleaning in the region. As the demand grew, Keli embraced sustainability, transitioning to eco-conscious cleaning products and techniques, becoming a pioneer in environmentally responsible cleaning. Expanding their reach, Keli and her team established branches in neighboring areas, providing exceptional services to a broader clientele. Staying at the forefront of the industry, Keli Cristina Cleaning Services embraced innovation and technology, optimizing efficiency and offering a personalized approach to clients. Empowering employees through training and skill development, the company fostered a positive work environment that translated into a motivated team approaching each assignment with passion and professionalism. Looking ahead, Keli Cristina Cleaning Services envisions a future where they become the benchmark of excellence in the cleaning industry. They plan to expand their eco-friendly initiatives and make a global impact by spreading the message of sustainability and compassion. In Keli's words, "Cleaning is not just a task; it is an act of care. Our story is one of compassion, integrity, and dedication. We are not just cleaners; we are dream enablers, creating spaces where dreams thrive, and lives flourish."

Weekly Cleaning Service

Our Weekly Cleaning Services is designed to keep your home or office consistently clean and organized. Our professional team will visit your property once a week, ensuring that it remains fresh and comfortable.

The service includes:

  • 1. Vacuuming and Mopping All Floors.
  • 2. Dusting All Surfaces, Including Furniture and Decor.
  • 3. Cleaning and Sanitizing Bathrooms.
  • 4. Wiping Down Kitchen Countertops, Appliances, and Cabinets.
  • 5. Emptying and Sanitizing Trash Bins.
  • 6. Making Beds and Changing Bed Linens (if requested).
  • 7. Organizing Common Areas and Tidying Up Spaces.
  • Please click on "Estimate" for a free estimate.

Price Range: $120 - $180 per visit

Bi-Weekly Cleaning Service

Our bi-weekly cleaning service is designed for customers who want thorough cleaning on alternate weeks. With bi-weekly visits, we ensure that your property remains fresh and well-maintained throughout the month.

Tasks Included:

  • Tasks included in weekly cleaning
  • Cleaning baseboards, vents, and ceiling fans
  • Cleaning interior windows and window sills
  • Dusting blinds and curtains
  • Polishing doorknobs and light switches
  • Removing cobwebs from corners and ceilings

Price Range: $150 - $250 per visit

Monthly Cleaning Service

Our monthly cleaning service offers a more comprehensive and deep cleaning session to address less frequently cleaned areas. This service is ideal for maintaining a high level of cleanliness and hygiene.

Tasks Included:

  • Tasks included in bi-weekly cleaning
  • Cleaning and dusting high shelves, ledges, and light fixtures
  • Cleaning behind and under furniture (moveable furniture)
  • Vacuuming and cleaning upholstery and curtains
  • Cleaning interior of kitchen appliances, such as the oven and refrigerator
  • Cleaning interior and exterior of kitchen cabinets
  • Washing interior windows and glass doors
  • Deep cleaning of hard-to-reach areas, such as behind heavy furniture

Price Range: $200 - $350 per visit
